Join the First European LGBTQIA+ Convention of Narcotics Anonymous

The first-ever European LGBTQIA+ convention of Narcotics Anonymous will take place in Paris from June 27th to 29th, 2025. This event is an opportunity for fellowship, unity, and celebrating diversity within the Narcotics Anonymous community!

Location:

Espace Saint-Martin, 199bis rue Saint-Martin Paris, France

Program:

Friday: Welcome meeting

Saturday: Recovery meetings all day, public information event during the Paris Pride March, fellowship + fun walk around Paris' famous landmarks!

Sunday: Main convention day: speaker jams all day + talent show! TBC: clean party

Tickets:

First tickets opening on Monday January, 6th at 4pm CET.
